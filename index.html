Ayrton Senna

The fastest driver in history

Here's a time line of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • Mar 21, 1960 - Ayrton Senna was born in Brazil.
  • Jan 1, 1964 - He started carting.
  • Jan 2, 1981 - Ayrton Senna left college and went to England for racing.
  • Oct 2, 1981 - He won 13 of 18 formula ford racing.
  • Oct 2, 1982 - He bacame the champion of Formula Ford 2000.
  • Oct 2, 1983 - He became the champion of British Formula 3 racing.
  • Mar 2, 1984 - He started to race in Formula 1.
  • Oct 2, 1988 - He bacame champion of Formula 1.
  • Oct 2, 1990 – Again he bacame champion of Formula 1.
  • Oct 2, 1991 – And again he bacame champion of Formula 1.
  • May 1, 1994 - He died in an accident during Italian Grand Prix.
